STUDENT PARTICIPANTS PROFILE AND BENEFITS

| Eligibility

Participants will be invited from a pool of students majoring in Geosciences, with a GPA between 2.6 and 3.0

| Commitment

Each student participant will sign the letter of commitment

| Duties

  • Consistent active participation in all activities
  • Completion of all assigned rotations
  • Commitment of 8-10 hours per week
  • On-time completion of all assignments
  • Maintain academic performance (GPA cannot fall below starting value)
  • Honest participation in all evaluation surveys and interviews
  • Positive attitude
  • Demonstration of ability to perform in team/group activities

| Benefits

  • Professional career development
  • Real geoscience work experience
  • Exposure to working professionals
  • Professional and academic networking
  • Research experience
  • $3000 stipend for the academic rotation
  • $3000 stipend for each professional rotation (if selected)
  • 3 hours academic credit for the academic rotation
  • 3 hours academic credit for each professional rotation (if selected)
